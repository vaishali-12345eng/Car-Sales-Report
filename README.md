# Car-Sales-Report
The Car Sales Performance Dashboard gives a complete and visually effective interface to business data, allowing users to analyze sales performance, pricing, as well as geographical distribution in a real-time setting. The dashboard helps in making informed business decisions by pointing out key business components in a simple and organized manner.

The Sales Overview segment points out the major financial key performance indicators, which include the Year to Date (YTD) Total Sales, the Month to Date (MTD) Sales, the Year over Year (YoY) Growth, as well as deviations in the manner compared to the Previous Year to Date (PTYD) performance.
The Average Price Analysis section gives a thorough understanding of pricing trends, which highlights the YTD, MTD average selling price, YoY changes, and the difference in prices offered in the previous year.
The dashboard also provides Car Sales Metrics for cars, featuring a set of metrics to provide information on the number of cars sold on YTD, MTD bases and YoY % change, also compared to PTYD.
